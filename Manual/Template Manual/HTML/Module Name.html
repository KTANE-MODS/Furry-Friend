2. Time to Hangout!

  • Now you know what time to hangout with each friend. You are going to press the friend in order of morning, evening, then night when you have the option to choose who to hangout with.
  • Once you choose your friend, a dialogue box will appear along with a stamina bar.
  • For each friend, there will always be a choice of where to go, two scenarios, another choice of where to go, and another two scenarios. Get through these to progress through the module.
  • You will utilize their dialogue, description, and special conditions to make sure your friend is happy with what you decide to do. Keep in mind that you are allowed to make wrong choices without too much punishment. But your friend will keep in mind what you choose that will either please or displease them.
  • Certain choices require you to use some of your stamina. You start with 10 stamina and running out will limit your options. Each choice will state the amount of stamina you will use. You will gain 1 stamina every 2 scenarios.
  • Once you make all choices for a scenario, if you made your friend displeased, part of your heart will break and it will cause a strike but you will still move on with the day.
  • Every time you have a choice to choose where to go, you have an extra option of booping your friend's nose. Press their nose to boop UwU. Depending on which friend it is, either a special event will happen due to a special condition, or the friend will be more prone to enjoy what choices you make if there is not one. You can only do this ONCE.
  • A friend’s Special Condition can apply but IF the serial number contains a letter in their full name (NOT their short names). Their full name will always be on the top of their page.
  • Once you have hung out with all 3 fur friends, the module will solve.

The following next pages are all of the descriptions of the fur friends and their special conditions.

Aaron Kitty Boiii

Pronouns: He/Him

Type: Feline

Aaron is an energetic maine coon who wants to love, and help out anyone wherever he goes. His appearance takes the form of a humanoid creature with cat features like ears, paws, and a tail. To him, he’s classified as being an “Emo-Fem-Cat-Boi.” He has black hair, always seems to wear a collar, and seems to favor wearing full black clothing. He also always wears red shoes for some reason. His cat features are usually colored black, white, and pink.

Depending on who Aaron is with and the environments he is in, he is either energetic, or really quiet, but always maintains a low key personality. He’s prone to be energetic if he is surrounded by people he is familiar with, quiet settings, or things he really enjoys. He’s prone to be really quiet if basically he is in settings of the opposite of being energetic. Like, unfamiliar people, loud settings, or displeasure of where he currently is. He doesn’t mind a lot of things he doesn’t like, because both traits are who he really is, and further emulates a low key personality. The most special thing about Aaron is that he always wants to love and help wherever he goes. No matter how people treat him, who they are, and the circumstances; Aaron wants to always somehow love and help.

Aaron is a very introverted person, so he is always into those activities. He likes to draw, play musical instruments, and play video games. He aspires to be an animator. He really likes physical touch with his favorite being hugs. He loves being creative and solving problems, most prominently math related. His favorite place to go is the amusement park. He dislikes sports, insects, and public bathrooms. Aaron doesn’t like the competitive mind and rather uses his litter box at home.

Because Aaron is a cat, his cat instincts always take over. He doesn’t really talk like a normal human, and he tends to slur his words, so hearing him might be a little challenging. If there is a ball, tail, or laser around he will instantly get distracted. Because of his speech and cat instincts, are why people tend to hate him. But he does have an evil side, secretly plotting to take over the world one day! Muahahaha!

SPECIAL CONDITION: If the bomb has the modules “nya~” AND “Flyswatting,” boop Aaron’s nose to engage in a work session with him! You will only have to make a few choices for the entirety of Aaron.

Eltrick

Pronouns: He/Him/She/Her/They/Them

Type: Fractal?

Eltrick is generally an uninteresting person and doesn’t know if that’s a good or bad thing. Their appearance is a basic human body with their head being a fractal while wearing a blue shirt and black pants. They don’t have eyes, nose, mouth, and ears but still are able to communicate normally.

With a desire to learn, Eltrick is an impressively intelligent individual. They’re passionate about problem solving and prefer to be put in places where they have to think. But they unfortunately lack normal communication and social skills. They’re unable to hold a solid conversation with anyone, due to being very shy and uninteresting. Eltrick knows this major fault about themself, but makes up for being smart.

Eltrick obviously likes math and logic, using their proficient skills as a perk. They like staying organized and really like doing puzzles, especially the ones that make them think. They for some reason have a weird quirk of enjoying a teeny bit of physical pain, don’t ask. The thing that Eltrick hates the most is annoying people! They hate mostly naturally angry people and unconstructive or pointless arguments. They also dislike long conversations and being in uncomfortable situations.

They want to avoid those at all costs!

Now I know your asking it, Eltrick is definitely a furry.

Kugel

Pronouns: He/Him

Type: Protogen

Kugel is a chill bean that loves socializing and being a logistical nerd. His appearance is a robotic protogen with his fur mostly consisting of blue and cyan. He’s often seen being energetic, funny, competitive, and just sooo cuddddllyy! Just remember that he loves being called FURRY TRASH.

Kugel really likes having company and being around his friends, so he’s usually nice but sarcastic. Being a literal computer, he always likes to nerd out whenever he gets the opportunity and often scrutinizes people on their logic and intelligence, partially as a joke. He goes to school majoring in Computer Science. He often wants to do his own thing, whether that’s engaging in challenges with no reward or hanging out in what he calls many different “Niche” communities. Despite these cool traits, he can be quite stupid, lazy, and impulsive at times. Aaron thinks it’s just a minor bug in his coding.

Kugel likes all kinds of subjects one learns in school, like Geography, Math, English, Science, and more! Kugel likes cheese and solving puzzling problems, like his favorite rubik’s cube. Being a LITERAL COMPUTER again, he loves programming and cryptography. Kugel dislikes overly loud noises, but also dislikes silence. He dislikes bugs, surprises, and making mistakes so just avoid those. His worst enemy is his long software updates… Hopefully that doesn’t happen.

Unfortunately, you won’t be able to see many of these traits, due to a… language barrier. Kugel is only programmed to talk in binary, so you’ll have to translate what he is saying. Luckily he helps you by saying things as short as possible. You’ll be translating the binary to base 36.

Kugel is a protogen that takes a lot of power. The less batteries there are on the bomb, the more you’re required to charge Kugel. If Kugel starts to shut down, use 1 stamina to charge him up to continue the scenario. Failing to charge him will result in the end of the scenario with a strike.

SPECIAL CONDITION: If the bomb has 6+ batteries and has the module “Kugelblitz,” boop his nose to put Kugel to Sleep Mode. Leave him there to rot and skip all of his scenarios entirely!
